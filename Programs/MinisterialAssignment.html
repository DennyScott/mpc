Manitoba Police Commission - Ministerial Assignment

Ministerial Assignment

As part of its duties under The Police Services Act, the Manitoba Police Commission will be called upon from time to time by the Minister to conduct research and/or provide recommendations on any issues affecting or related to law enforcement and policing. The Manitoba Police Commission is committed in these cases, to collaborating, when appropriate, with both affected stakeholders and the public.
